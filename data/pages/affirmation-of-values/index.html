The University of Virginia Library is an open and inclusive community that embraces the full spectrum of human attributes, perspectives, and cultures.

The values of respect, integrity, diversity and inclusion, collaboration, and innovation guide our organization, our behavior, and our interactions.

We recognize the dignity of others in both in public and private discourse. We value openness, fairness, and trustworthiness. We encourage and celebrate our differences, seeking out numerous perspectives, including those that challenge the status quo.

We are committed to the academic success of our diverse community, and we work hard to promote understanding and inclusion through librarianship.

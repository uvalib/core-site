Library computers are provided for research and academic work. Priority is given to UVA students, faculty, and staff for educational purposes.

Please see Library staff about access to computers.  Guest access may be limited to specific computers in some libraries.

Please limit your computer use to one hour per day. Guests younger than 16 must be accompanied by an adult. Guest users will be asked to present an ID.

Library staff may request you vacate the computer if you appear to be playing a game, watching a TV show or some other non-academic activity.

Actions such as downloading copyrighted or illegal content are strictly forbidden.  You will be reported to the UVA police if you engage in these activities.

See Library staff for special research arrangements.

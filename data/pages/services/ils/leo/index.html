Overview

The LEO service provides service to UVA faculty members by delivering library material for personal research use to departmental offices. Delivered library material includes items retrieved from a UVA library, or via Interlibrary Loan.

  Service Alert: We are expanding! Construction of the Ivy Stacks addition, an expansion of our off-site storage facility, begins in summer 2017. That means that you may see delays for materials ordered from our Ivy Stacks location. We will do all we can to deliver your materials in a timely manner. Thank you for your patience!

We will update this site as more information about service changes resulting from construction becomes available. Thank you for your patience!

Request Process

  • First-time users, please visit our sign in page to set up an account. After setting up your account, you will be able to request and track the process of all your Interlibrary Services requests.
  • To request materials from Virgo:
    • To request an article or book, visit the Virgo catalog to search for your material.
    • After viewing the details of your selection, click 'Request LEO delivery (faculty/SCPS)'. You will be prompted to log in, if you haven't already. Select 'Submit Request' and you are done.
  • To request materials not owned by a UVA Library:
    • Logon to your ILS account and choose the appropriate Request Type from the menu on the left side of the page. Fill in as much information as you have available to ensure we borrow the correct item. (Faculty requests will automatically be delivered to the office of the department unless you have opted for delivery to a Library.)

Limitations

Certain types of materials cannot be delivered via LEO. These materials include items like those made "in-library use only" by lending libraries, items from the Reference collection, and microfiche.

Delivery of Material

Articles will be delivered via e-mail in PDF format.

Books will be delivered to departments for Faculty.

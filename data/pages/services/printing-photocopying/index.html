Printer Management & Servicing

  • Printing and copying in the libraries are managed by Printing and Copying Services (PCS).
  • See Public Printers for detailed information, or Public Printer Tips to avoid common problems.
  • You may report service issues directly to PCS.

Printers and Copiers Available in the Libraries

  • Photocopy machines and black and white printers are located in every library.

Color printers are available in:

  • Alderman Library (Scholars’ Lab and 3rd floor)
  • Clemons Library (4th floor)
  • Charles L. Brown Science & Engineering Library (main floor)
  • Fine Arts Library

Ask at any library service desk for specific locations.

PrintMe

  • PrintMe printers are available in Alderman, Clemons, and Brown Science & Engineering. You can print from Box, Dropbox, Google Drive and USB. Print jobs can also be:
    • uploaded at PrintMe.com, and
    • emailed from iOS and Android devices to hoosprinting@printme.com.

To print from Library computers:

  1. Select File > Print  (Should default to EFT Printme)
  2. Enter email (when asked)
  3. Write down code or check email or App for barcode
  4. Proceed to self-serve print station with code and follow on screen directions
  5. Select the document you want to print
  6. Select print option
  7. Review pricing and approve printing
  8. Complete transaction or go to main menu to select another feature.

Setting up your laptop to print

  • Download the PrintMe app for easy printing from your laptop.  You can also just email your document to hoosprinting.printme.com !

Double-Sided Printing

  • Duplexing printing is available in Alderman (4th floor and Scholars’ Lab), Brown Science & Engineering, and Clemons.

  • To print double sided from our public computers one must, oddly, chose Single sided on the computer.
    When you get to the print kiosk you pick 1-sided to get to the option to print double-sided.

Costs

  • Black & white printing and copying (8.5″ x 11″) cost $.08 per copy.
  • Color printing (8.5″ x 11″) costs $.60 per copy.

Payment Methods

  • Cavalier Advantage cards are needed for all printing and copying.

Purchase or add money to a Cavalier Advantage card at a vending machine in:

  • Alderman
  • Clemons
  • Fine Arts
  • Brown Science and Engineering

Or, make a deposit to your Cavalier Advantage card online or via phone or mail.

Need help finding credible sources for your upcoming paper? Need to know how to cite those sources? Has your teacher banned Wikipedia?  Meet with one of the SourceDorks! The SourceDorks will help you find sources relevant to your paper, narrow down a big topic, get peer-reviewed sources, and use sources your teacher will love!

Drop in and visit the SourceDorks in Clemons Library on the second floor to get experienced help with your research!

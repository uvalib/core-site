The Library is a contact point for finding Media Accessibility Resources such as those listed below. We can direct you to appropriate resources for creating accessible media, but we do not currently provide other services in this area.

Here you can find:

  • Captioning Basics
  • UVA-approved vendors who can caption existing digital media
  • Information about federal and Virginia state laws regarding accessibility and media
  • Video Description Information

For more information, contact Leigh Rockey, Media Collections Librarian, at lar4k@virginia.edu.

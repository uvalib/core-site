Clemons Room 320 and Clark Room G-043 (located in Brown Library) are outfitted with immersive Cisco TelePresence systems. This allows participants to interact with colleagues at other institutions via full-size, interactive audio and video. Use of this room and technology must be in conjunction with comparable telepresence partners. Requests for reservations of these rooms will be subject to approval by the office of the Associate Provost for Academic Support and Classroom Management.

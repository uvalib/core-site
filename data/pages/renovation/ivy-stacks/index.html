Ivy Stacks is a high-density storage and retrieval facility located one mile west of Alderman Library. It currently holds over one million books and other materials from the University of Virginia’s libraries, all of which are available for checkout through VIRGO.

An expansion of the facility is underway [ WATCH LIVE ] and, by spring 2018, the number of items that can be housed there will double. Additionally, an on-site reading room is being constructed for researchers. Ivy Stacks will also be outfitted with solar panels to reduce its energy footprint. We will be retrieving materials from Ivy Stacks during this construction.
